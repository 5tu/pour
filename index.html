The 4:6 method was developed by Tetsu Kasuya, the 2016 World Brewers Cup Champion. This method emphasizes the balance of sweetness, acidity, and strength in your coffee by dividing your total water amount into a 4:6 ratio. The first 40% of the water is used to control sweetness and acidity, while the remaining 60% adjusts the strength.
